This is a portfolio site for my musical project, 'Ryan Maguire'. It's a place for audiences to enjoy my music and videos, and provides press photos and information and a contact form.

## Features

- **Responsiveness**
- **Parallax Image**
- **Media Player**
- **Dark/Light Mode**
- **Image Carousel**
- **Contact Form**


## Technologies

- **HTML**
- **SCSS** - Using BEM and ITCSS methodologies
- **JavaScript**
